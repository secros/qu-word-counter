Frequently Asked Questions

How many words is a 5 minute read?

At an average reading speed of 200–250 words per minute, a 5 minute read is approximately 1,000–1,250 words. This tool calculates reading time automatically based on 225 words per minute.

How many characters is a tweet?

Twitter/X allows up to 280 characters per post for standard accounts. Click the "Twitter/X" limit button above to track your count in real time.

How many characters should a meta description be?

Google typically displays meta descriptions up to 155–160 characters. Anything longer gets truncated in search results. Use the "Meta desc" limit button above to stay within range.

Does this tool count spaces as characters?

Yes — the "Characters" count includes spaces. The "No spaces" count excludes them. Both are shown above so you can use whichever metric your platform requires.

How many words is a 1,000 character passage?

A 1,000 character passage contains approximately 150–170 words, depending on average word length. English words average around 5–6 characters including spaces.
