About This Tool

What this tool does

The Word & Character Counter instantly counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading and speaking time as you type or paste text.

Who it's for

Writers, students, bloggers, SEO professionals, and social media managers — anyone who needs to hit a word count, stay within a character limit, or estimate how long their content will take to read or deliver.

How it works

All counting happens live in your browser as you type. Nothing you enter is sent to a server or stored. The tool also supports platform-specific character limits — click Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Meta description, or Title tag to see a live progress bar against that limit.

Reading and speaking time

Reading time is estimated at 225 words per minute, which is the average adult reading speed for on-screen text. Speaking time is estimated at 130 words per minute, which is a comfortable presentation pace.
